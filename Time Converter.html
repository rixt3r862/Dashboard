What it accepts

Use whichever format is fastest for you. The helper will clean it up and convert it.

00:00 -> 12:00 AM Midnight stays in the AM block.
12:00 -> 12:00 PM Noon stays 12, but flips to PM.

Quick Reference

Tap any card to drop that time into the converter.

Fast Rules

00:00 to 11:59 These stay in AM.
12:00 to 23:59 These switch to PM.
13:00 and up Subtract 12 from the hour for the 12-hour version.

Tip: three digits like 905 are read as 09:05, and four digits like 1717 are read as 17:17.
