Table Rules

Automatic meld scoring first slice.

  • Eleven rounds are dealt from 3 cards through 13 cards.
  • The wild rank matches the hand size for the round.
  • Draw from the stock or discard pile, then discard one card.
  • Books and runs need at least three cards.
  • Going out gives every other player one final turn.
  • Lowest total score wins after the king round.
