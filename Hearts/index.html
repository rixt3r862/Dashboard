Table Rules

Standard 52-card Hearts.

  • 2 of clubs leads the first trick.
  • Follow suit when possible.
  • No hearts or queen of spades on the first trick if avoidable.
  • Hearts cannot be led until broken.
  • Each heart is 1 point; queen of spades is 13.
  • Shooting the moon gives every opponent 26.

Scores - Lowest score wins when someone reaches the target.
