Table Rules

Classic/simple first slice.

  • Five cards are dealt to every player.
  • Play by matching the current suit or rank.
  • 8s are wild and declare the next suit.
  • If blocked, draw up to five cards until you can play.
  • Pass only after five draws or when the draw pile is exhausted.
  • The round ends when a player empties their hand.
  • The round winner scores all opponents' hand points.
