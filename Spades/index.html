Table Rules

Partnership Spades, fixed teams.

  • You partner with the player across the table.
  • Bid 0 through 13 before each hand.
  • Follow suit when possible.
  • Spades cannot be led until broken unless you only have spades.
  • Made bids score 10 per bid plus bags. Missed bids lose 10 per bid.
  • Successful nil scores 100; failed nil loses 100.
  • Every 10 team bags costs 100.

Scores - Highest score wins when a team reaches the target.
